I am a Data Scientist with a background in applied data science and statistics from the University of Rennes 1, with several years of professional experience in consulting and industry. I have worked on end-to-end data projects involving analysis, modeling and decision support, with a strong focus on business impact and actionable insights.

During my professional experience as a Data Scientist at KPMG Corporate Finance and as a consultant, I contributed to a wide range of use cases including customer segmentation, churn analysis, RFM and retention studies, predictive modeling and process automation. I also designed dashboards and decision-support tools to help stakeholders better leverage their data.

In parallel, my roles as a mentor at OpenClassrooms and teaching assistant at Jedha have strengthened my ability to structure data problems, justify modeling choices and communicate results clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences. I am particularly interested in data-driven products, analytics and applied machine learning projects where models must be interpretable, robust and useful for real-world decision-making.
